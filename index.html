Harry Yang

Engineer, Researcher and Entrepreneur

I am a Professor at HKUST AMC and a visiting Professor at Yale University. My research focuses on the advancement of AGI, particularly in image and video generation. Previously, I worked at Meta as a Research Scientist. I am also the co-founder of Nesa. I earned my PhD from USC and completed my undergraduate studies at USTC (SCGY). I am honored to be a recipient of the USC Distinguished Alumni Award.
