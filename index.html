I am a Professor at HKUST AMC and a Visiting Professor at Yale University. My research focuses on advancing AGI, particularly in image and video generation. I am also interested in distributed systems, privacy, efficiency, and the intersection of AI and Web3. Previously, I worked at Meta as a Research Scientist. I am also the co-founder of Nesa. I earned PhD in computer science from USC and completed my undergraduate studies at USTC (SCGY). I am honored to be a recipient of the USC Distinguished Alumni Award.
